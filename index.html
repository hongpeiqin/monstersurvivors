About Monster Survivors

Monster Survivors is an action-packed survival game where you face endless waves of monsters. Your goal is simple: survive as long as possible while defeating monsters and collecting power-ups to become stronger.

As you progress, the difficulty increases, and you'll need to strategically choose upgrades and navigate the environment to stay alive. With its addictive gameplay and progression system, Monster Survivors offers hours of challenging entertainment.

Game Details

  • Genre: Survival, Action, Roguelike
  • Players: Single Player
  • Controls: WASD or Arrow Keys to move, Mouse to aim and attack
  • Difficulty: Progressively challenging
  • Play Time: 10-30 minutes per run

Survival Tips

  1. Keep moving - Standing still is a death sentence. Always stay on the move to avoid being surrounded.
  2. Prioritize upgrades - Focus on damage and area-of-effect upgrades early to clear enemies faster.
  3. Collect experience orbs - Don't miss experience orbs dropped by defeated monsters, as they're crucial for leveling up.
  4. Use special abilities wisely - Save your special abilities for when you're surrounded or facing tough enemies.
  5. Learn enemy patterns - Different monsters have different attack patterns. Learning these will help you survive longer.
